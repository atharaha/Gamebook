Welcome to Gamebook!

What is a Gamebook?

A Gamebook, as defined by Wikipedia, is “a work of fiction that allows the reader to participate in the story by making effective choices. The narrative branches along various paths through the use of numbered paragraphs or pages.” (Wikipedia, 2012). Gamebooks came into existence during the last century and grew to popularity during the role playing game craze. Some notable Gamebooks are the Choose Your Own Adventure series created by R.A. Montgomery, the Fighting Fantasy series created by Steve Jackson and Ian Livingstone and the Give Yourself Goosebumps series by R.L. Stine.

So, What Does Our Website Do?

Gamebook is a site where users can share their Gamebooks with one another. Anyone can read the Gamebooks published on the site, but on signing up for an account they can create their own Gamebooks. We hope you enjoy reading and sharing Gamebooks on our website.
